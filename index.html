Our Journey to South Africa

üåç

Saravana Bhavan's expansion to South Africa represents more than just a business opportunity‚Äîit's about bringing authentic South Indian vegetarian cuisine to a vibrant, diverse market that has long awaited quality Indian dining experiences.

Why Durban? South Africa's Indian community, particularly in Durban, represents one of the largest Indian populations outside of India. With over 1.5 million people of Indian descent and a growing appreciation for authentic vegetarian cuisine, the market presented the perfect opportunity for Saravana Bhavan to establish its first African location.

Our mission is to serve the community with the same authentic recipes and traditional preparation methods that have made Saravana Bhavan a household name across India and the world. From our signature Masala Dosa to aromatic Biryani, every dish honors our heritage while embracing the multicultural spirit of South Africa.
